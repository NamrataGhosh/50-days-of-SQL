24. https://leetcode.com/problems/user-activity-for-the-past-30-days-i/description/?envType=study-plan-v2&envId=top-sql-50

Answer :   select activity_date as day, count(distinct user_id) as active_users
from Activity
where activity_date >= '2019-06-28' and activity_date <= '2019-07-27' group by activity_date;
