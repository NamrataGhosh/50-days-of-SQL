8.Question:https://leetcode.com/problems/customer-who-visited-but-did-not-make-any-transactions/submissions/1712463857/?envType=study-plan-v2&envId=top-sql-50  

Answer : SELECT v.customer_id, COUNT(*) AS count_no_trans
	 FROM Visits v LEFT JOIN Transactions t
	 ON v.visit_id = t.visit_id 
	 WHERE t.transaction_id IS NULL
	 GROUP BY v.customer_id;
