29. https://leetcode.com/problems/consecutive-numbers/?envType=study-plan-v2&envId=top-sql-50

Answer :   select distinct l1.num as ConsecutiveNums
from logs l1
join logs l2 on l1.id = l2.id - 1
join logs l3 on l1.id = l3.id - 2
where l1.num = l2.num
  and l2.num = l3.num;
