16. https://leetcode.com/problems/average-selling-price/submissions/1724658641/?envType=study-plan-v2&envId=top-sql-50

Answer :    SELECT p.product_id,ROUND(IFNULL(SUM(u.units * p.price) / SUM(u.units), 0),2) AS average_price
	    FROM Prices p
	    LEFT JOIN UnitsSold u
	    ON p.product_id = u.product_id AND u.purchase_date BETWEEN p.start_date AND p.end_date
	    GROUP BY p.product_id;
