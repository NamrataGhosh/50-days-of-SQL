18. https://leetcode.com/problems/percentage-of-users-attended-a-contest/?envType=study-plan-v2&envId=top-sql-50

Answer :    SELECT contest_id, ROUND(COUNT(user_id) * 100.0 / (SELECT COUNT(*) FROM Users), 2) AS percentage
	    FROM Register
	    GROUP BY contest_id
	    ORDER BY percentage DESC, contest_id ASC;
