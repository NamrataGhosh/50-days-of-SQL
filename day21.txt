21. https://leetcode.com/problems/immediate-food-delivery-ii/?envType=study-plan-v2&envId=top-sql-50

Answer :   select round(sum(case when order_date = customer_pref_delivery_date then 1 else 0 end)*100.0/count(*),2) as immediate_percentage
	   from Delivery
 	   where (customer_id, order_date) in (
    		select customer_id, MIN(order_date)
    		from Delivery
    		group by customer_id
	   );
